Create Assessment!

  1. Click Add Another Question button to start creating the assessment.
  2. Enter the Question.
  3. Enter the options for the question.
  4. If you wish to give more than two options, click on the check-box provided.
  5. Once done entering the options, please select the correct answer.
  6. Enter the marks for that question.
  7. If you wish to keep adding questions to the assessment, click Add Another Question button.
  8. If you wish to submit, enter the submit button.
  9. To cancel the assessment, click Cancel button.
